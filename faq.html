How to start writing content for my website?

Let someone interview you. By a friend or colleague. It's playful and fun! Ans it sharpens your mind to pitch your text on paper! Write down the questions and answers. Build a storytelling text of it. Imagine what pictures could illustrate your info or what kind of video images! In this order: Introduction, why, where how and when and a conclusion that makes your audience contact you or buy your product/service. The call for action. Make someone review your text. Think also of SEO when writing your text.
